Understanding CKD

Chronic Kidney Disease affects millions worldwide. Early detection and treatment can help manage symptoms and slow disease progression.

Key Risk Factors

  • High Blood Pressure

    Can damage blood vessels in the kidneys

  • Diabetes

    May cause kidney damage over time

  • Heart Disease

    Affects blood flow to the kidneys

  • Smoking

    Increases risk of kidney disease
